Biography

Linus Torvalds is a Finnish-American software engineer who is the creator and principle deeloper of the Linus Kennel, which is the kenel for a number of operating systems. He also created the version control system Git. Torvalds was born on December 28, 1969 in Helsinki, Finland. He studied comouter science at the University of Helsinki, and he developed the Linus Kernel as a student project in 1991, Since then, the Linus Kernel has become one of the most widely used Kernels in the world, powering everything from smartphone to supercomputers.
